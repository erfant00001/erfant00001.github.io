GINOT is a geometry-aware neural-operator (DeepONet-style) surrogate that predicts steady blood flow in branching vessel geometries directly from the geometry, without re-meshing or re-solving. It is built on **NVIDIA PhysicsNeMo** and trained against **COMSOL** CFD, enabling near-instant flow and pressure predictions for previously unseen vessel shapes.

*Code is currently in a private repository and available on request.*
