This pipeline turns a computed blood-flow field into a patient-specific absorbed-dose map for Yttrium-90 radioembolization, a targeted therapy for liver tumors. It takes the CFD/PINN flow split, distributes radioactive microspheres, and convolves the deposition with a dose-point kernel to produce a three-dimensional absorbed-dose map in Gray (Gy).

*Code is currently in a private repository and available on request.*
